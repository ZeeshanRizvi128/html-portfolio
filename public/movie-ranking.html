The Best Movies according to Zeeshan

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Spider Man 2

The perfect superhero movie. It has great action and drama,
and it really cuts deep into the core of who Spider-Man is.

Spider Man: Into the Spider-verse

This movie was made by Spider-Man fans, for Spider-Man fans.
With so many great refernces to the characters history, it builds
upon as well as adds to the character in a way no movie or franchise
has ever done.

Spider Man 1

Without this movie, modern superhero movies wouldnt be as popular.
This movie proved that superhero movies could be serious, action packed,
and well made; all while delivering an amazing origin story, introducing him
to many generations to come.
